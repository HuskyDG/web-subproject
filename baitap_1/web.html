One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluting his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chanee in the courts. The Street Lawycr is a great book. It is well written and inferesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
